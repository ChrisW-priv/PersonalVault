title: Homogenous equations
---

Given any ODE of the first order:

1. Let $y=vx$

2. Differentiate both sides using product rule $$dy=x \cdot dv + v \cdot dx$$

3. Substitute
4. Simplify
5. Separation of Variables
6. Substitute back $v=\frac{x}{y}$
