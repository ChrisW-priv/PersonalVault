title: Separation of Variables
---

Easiest way of solving ODEs

Step 1: Change the equation to the form

$$F(y)dy = G(x)dx$$

Step 2: Integrate both sides

$$\int F(y)dy = \int G(x)dx$$

Step 3: Solve this new equation like any other
