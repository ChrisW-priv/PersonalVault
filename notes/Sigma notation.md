## Example

"Sum all numbers from 1 to 10" would be

$$\sum_{1}^{10}n = 1 + 2 + \dots + 10$$

"Sum all positive numbers" is 

$$\sum_{1}^{\infty}n = 1+2+3+\dots$$

 > [!Notice]
 > in markdown and latex instead of `/Sigma_1^{10}` we write it as `/sum_1^{10}`
